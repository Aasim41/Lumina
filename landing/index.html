Installation Guide

1 Pause Google Play Protect

Because Lumina is not on the Google Play Store yet, Google Play Protect will flag it as an unknown app during installation. You must pause it to install.

Open Google Play Store → Tap your profile icon → Play Protect → Settings (gear icon) → Turn off "Scan apps with Play Protect".

2 Enable SMS Tracking (Android 13+)

Android blocks sensitive permissions for apps downloaded outside the Play Store. To enable automated SMS expense tracking, you must manually allow the setting.

  1. Open your Android Settings app.
  2. Go to Apps and select Lumina.
  3. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner.
  4. Select Allow restricted settings and confirm with your fingerprint/PIN.
  5. Open Lumina, tap the Sync SMS button, and allow the permission!
